Phase 9: The Artifact and Story

Backstory

Oxygen is becoming scarce on planet Earth, no one knows why it’s been happening. Our most oxygen producing species are dying off. The oceans and air have become hot and dry, it is no longer a sustainable environment. The unexpected happens, aliens have come from space looking for a new home. They mean no harm, they are an advanced species that have come to help. Their bodies emit oxygen through a similar process of photosynthesis. They have come here because the sun near their home planet has died out and they hope to coexist with humans. The humans don't want to share their Earth with them and take matters into their own hands. They begin kidnapping the aliens and farming the oxygen. The rest of the aliens escape to look for a new home. The oxygen from the farmed aliens bring back moisture into the air and plants start to grow again.

The pod aliens are placed in to siphon the oxygen off their skin.
