Phase 3: Rough Backstory and Mockup

Oxygen is becoming scarce on planet Earth, no one knows why it’s been happening. Our most oxygen producing species are dying off. The oceans and air have become hot and dry, it is no longer a sustainable environment. Strange things have started to appear from space, aliens! They mean no harm, they are an advanced species that have come to help. Their bodies emit oxygen through a similar process of photosynthesis. They have come here because the sun near their home planet has died out and they hope to coexist with humans. Unfortunately, some humans don’t like the idea of sharing their planet and have begun kidnapping and farming the aliens for their oxygen.
