Arduino to Processing

In this project, there are 4 data channels on my micro box that affect a processing sketch. The toggle switch controls the object being drawn, it switches between a square and a circle. The photocell moves the object on the y-axis, at high light levels the object moves to the bottom of the screen. The photocell also controls the color of the neopixel and the color of the object. The slide pot controls the x-axis of the object. The momentary switch clears the processing sketch.
